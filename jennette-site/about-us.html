About us

Jennette Electric and Jennette Electric School is owned and operated by Michael P Jennette. I have been in the Electrical trade since 1964. I hold 5 Master licenses and 2 Journeyman Licenses. I started teaching for the Associated Builders and Contractors Association in1988 at the Blue Hill Regional Vocational High School as a night instructor.

I taught the Master and Journeyman course at Tri-County Regional Vocational High School at night in Franklin MA from 1993-2013. I am also a State Certified Provider for the 15 hour Massachusetts Continuing Education required by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, also with the states of New Hampshire and Maine and by agreement for Rhode Island.
